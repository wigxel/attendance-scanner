## How to Book InSpace

To request a booking, send an email to [business@inspace.ng](mailto:business@inspace.ng) with the following information:
1. Preferred date(s)
1. Preferred time(s)
1. Type of event or activity (e.g., meeting, workshop, seminar, training, photoshoot,networking event)
1. Expected number of attendees
1. Any equipment or facility requirements
1. Any other relevant information about your event

Upon receipt of your request, InSpace will review availability and provide a quotation, payment instructions, and any additional booking requirements.

A booking is not confirmed until:
- InSpace has issued written confirmation; and
- Any required payment has been received and verified.

Submitting a booking request does not guarantee availability.

## 1. Scope 

1.1. This policy governs the booking, rental and use of facilities operated by InSpace, located at 71 Rumuokuta Road, Mgbuoba, Obio/Akpor, Rivers State. 

1.2. This Policy applies to every individual, organisation, institution, business, or other entity ("Client") that books, rents, or otherwise uses any part of the InSpace Facility.

1.3. This Policy should be read together with the InSpace Terms & Conditions and any other applicable InSpace policies. Where there is any inconsistency, the terms of a separately executed Rental Agreement shall prevail for that specific booking.


## 2. Purpose

2.1. The purpose of this Policy is to:
- establish clear rules for the booking and use of the Facility;
- ensure the safety and comfort of all users;
- protect the Facility and its equipment;
- promote responsible and respectful use of shared spaces;
- define the rights and responsibilities of Clients and InSpace; and
- provide a consistent and professional rental experience.

## 4. Booking and Payment
### 4.1. **Booking Requests**

All booking requests must be submitted by email to [business@inspace.ng](mailto:business@inspace.ng). The booking request should include all information reasonably required by InSpace to assess availability and suitability.   Incomplete booking requests may delay processing.

### 4.2. **Booking Confirmation** 

A booking shall only be considered confirmed when:  
* InSpace has issued written confirmation; and
* all required payments have been received and verified.  
Until written confirmation is issued, InSpace reserves the right to accept another booking for the requested date.

### 4.3. **Booking Acceptance**

InSpace reserves the right to decline, refuse, or cancel any booking where it reasonably believes that the proposed activity:  
* is unlawful;
* presents a safety or security risk;
* may damage the Facility;
* exceeds the Facility's operational capacity;
* is inconsistent with the intended purpose or values of InSpace;
* may disturb neighbouring occupants; or
* is otherwise considered unsuitable for the Facility.
The reasons for declining a booking may be communicated at InSpace's discretion.

### 4.4. **Deposits and Fees**  
Unless otherwise agreed in writing by InSpace, the following payment terms shall apply.

#### 4.4.1.  **Standard Bookings**  

A deposit equal to fifty percent (50%) of the total rental fee is required to secure a booking where:  
* no promotional discount has been applied; and
* the total booking value is NGN 30,000 or more.  

The remaining balance shall be paid no later than three (3) calendar days before the scheduled booking date.

#### 4.4.2.  **Discounted and Small-Value Bookings**  

Where:  
- a promotional or negotiated discount has been applied; or
- the total booking value is less than NGN 30,000,  

full payment shall be required at the time of booking unless otherwise approved by InSpace.

#### 4.4.3.  **Proof of Payment**  

After each payment is made, the Client shall provide proof of payment (such as a transfer receipt or payment confirmation) by email to [business@inspace.ng](mailto:business@inspace.ng) for verification.

A booking shall not be regarded as fully paid until payment has been successfully verified by InSpace.

#### 4.4.4.  **Security Deposit**  

Depending on the nature, duration, value, or risk associated with a booking, InSpace may require a refundable security deposit.
The security deposit may be used to cover:
- damage to the Facility;
- loss of equipment;
- excessive cleaning;
- overtime charges;
- unpaid fees; or
- any other costs arising from the Client's use of the Facility.

Where no deductions are required, the security deposit shall be refunded within seven (7) business days after the booking.

#### 4.4.5.  **Additional Charges**

Additional charges may apply for, including but not limited to:
- overtime;
- excessive cleaning;
- damage to the Facility or equipment;
- exceeding the approved occupancy;
- replacement of lost items;
- unauthorised alterations to the Facility; or
- any additional services requested by the Client.
Such charges shall be payable upon demand by InSpace.

#### 4.4.6.  **Long-Term Bookings**  

Bookings extending beyond thirty (30) consecutive days, recurring bookings, or corporate partnerships may require a separate Space Rental Agreement.  

Where a separate agreement exists, its terms shall prevail over this Policy to the extent of any inconsistency.


#### 4.4.7.  **Rescheduling**  

Clients may request to reschedule a confirmed booking by submitting a written request to InSpace.  

Approval of any rescheduling request is entirely at the discretion of InSpace and shall be subject to availability.  

Where applicable, any payments already made may be transferred to the new booking date.  

InSpace reserves the right to charge an administrative fee for approved rescheduling requests.  

Requests submitted less than seven (7) days before the scheduled booking may be treated as cancellations.


#### 4.4.8.  **Cancellations and Refunds**

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, the following cancellation terms shall apply to paid bookings.
1. **Client Cancellations**
  Where the Client cancels
      1. Seven (7) or more calendar days before the booking:
        A cancellation fee equal to ten percent (10%) of the total amount paid shall apply.  
        The remaining balance shall be refunded.
    
      2. Less than seven (7) calendar days before the booking:
        All payments made shall be forfeited and no refund shall be issued.

2. **No-Shows**
    Clients who fail to attend their confirmed booking without prior notice shall not be entitled to any refund or credit.
                
3. **Free or Discounted Bookings**
    Cancellation terms for complimentary, sponsored, subsidised, or discounted bookings may differ and shall be communicated at the time of booking.

4. **Cancellation by InSpace**
    Where InSpace cancels a confirmed booking for reasons other than the Client's breach of this Policy, the Client may choose either:
  
      * a full refund of all amounts paid; or  
      * rescheduling of the booking to another mutually agreed date.

    InSpace shall not be liable for any indirect or consequential losses arising from such cancellation.

5. **Late Payments**
    Failure to make any required payment by the applicable due date may result in:
  
      * cancellation of the booking;  
      * suspension of access to the Facility;  
      * refusal of entry on the booking date; or  
      * cancellation of any discounts previously granted.

## 5. **Use of Facility**  
### 5.1. **Access, Hours, Check-In and Check-Out**
      
      Standard event hours at InSpace are **10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.**
      
      * Clients may not schedule events to start before 10:00 a.m. or end after 5:00 p.m., except where expressly agreed in writing by InSpace and subject to availability and any additional fees.
      
      * Clients may arrive and begin setup up to **1 hour before** their booked start time, but not earlier than 10:00 a.m.
      
      * All attendees, vendors, and belongings must be out of the space by the booked end time (or by 5:00 p.m., whichever is earlier), unless overtime has been approved.

### 5.2 **Overtime**
      Overtime is subject to prior approval and availability.
      
      * Where approved, overtime shall be charged at the prevailing overtime rate communicated by InSpace.  
      * Unless otherwise agreed in writing, overtime shall be charged at NGN 2,000 per hour.  
      * Any portion of an hour exceeding the booked period may, at the discretion of InSpace, be rounded up and charged as a full hour.  
      * Approval of overtime is not guaranteed and may be refused where another booking or operational requirement exists.  

### 5.3. **Permitted Uses**
      The Facility may only be used for lawful purposes approved by InSpace.
      Permitted activities include, but are not limited to:
        * Meetings
        * Photoshoots
        * Small events
        * Workshops
        * Trainings
        * Seminars 
        * Corporate events 
        * Networking events 
        * Interviews 
        * Business presentations 
        * Community events 
      Activities involving excessive noise, hazardous materials, open flames, live animals, or other unusual or high-risk activities require prior written approval.
      
      InSpace reserves the right to refuse activities considered unsuitable for the Facility.

### 5.4. **Capacity**
  The maximum occupancy of the Facility depends on the type of booking and the room configuration required.
  
  1. **Training Configuration**

      Where the Facility is configured as a training room using the standard training desks and chairs provided by InSpace, the maximum capacity is fifteen (15) participants.
      
      This limit is based on the available training furniture and is intended to ensure a comfortable and effective learning environment.
    
  2. **Event Configuration**

      For meetings, workshops, seminars, networking events, presentations, corporate events, and other approved activities where the Facility is configured as an event space, the maximum occupancy is thirty-five (35) persons, including organisers, speakers, facilitators, vendors, staff, and attendees.
      
      Event capacity may be achieved by reconfiguring the Facility and utilising seating from designated coworking areas, as determined by InSpace.
    
  3. **Approved Capacity**
      
      The maximum occupancy for each booking shall be specified in the booking confirmation. Clients shall not exceed the approved occupancy under any circumstances.
      
      Where the approved occupancy is exceeded, InSpace may require the number of occupants to be reduced immediately, charge additional fees where applicable, suspend the Event or terminate the booking without refund where the breach poses a safety or operational risk.

### 5.5. **Exclusive Use**
A booking grants the Client the right to use only the Space specified in the booking confirmation.

Unless expressly agreed otherwise, Clients shall not occupy or use any additional rooms or areas of the Facility.

Certain common areas may remain accessible to other authorised users of the Facility.

### 5.6 **Shared Spaces**

Clients shall respect all shared areas of the Facility, including reception areas, restrooms, corridors, entrances, parking areas, and other common spaces.

Clients shall ensure that their activities do not interfere with the normal operation of the Facility or the experience of other users.

### 5.7 **Parking**

Parking, where available, is provided on a first-come, first-served basis.

Availability of parking is not guaranteed.

Vehicles are parked entirely at the owner's risk.

InSpace accepts no responsibility for theft of, or damage to, vehicles or their contents.

### 5.8 **Accessibility**

Clients requiring reasonable accessibility accommodations are encouraged to notify InSpace before the booking date so that suitable arrangements may be considered where reasonably practicable.

### 5.9 **Lost Property**

Clients are responsible for removing all personal belongings at the end of their booking.

Any property left behind may be retained by InSpace for up to thirty (30) days.

After this period, unclaimed property may be disposed of, donated, or otherwise handled at the discretion of InSpace.

InSpace accepts no liability for personal belongings left within the Facility.

### 5.10. **Right of Entry**

Authorised representatives of InSpace may enter the booked Space at any reasonable time for the purposes of:

* ensuring compliance with this Policy; 

* responding to emergencies; 

* protecting persons or property; 

* carrying out maintenance or repairs where necessary; or 

* providing agreed support services. 

Except in emergencies, InSpace will endeavour to minimise disruption to the Client's Event.


## 6. **Responsibilities of the Client**  
### 6.1. **General Responsibility**

The Client is responsible for ensuring that all Attendees, facilitators, speakers, employees, contractors, volunteers, vendors, guests, and any other persons attending the Event comply with this Policy and all reasonable instructions issued by InSpace.

Any breach of this Policy by persons associated with the Client shall be deemed to be a breach by the Client.

 

### 6.2. **Conduct**

The Client shall ensure that all persons attending the Event conduct themselves in a respectful, orderly, and lawful manner.

The following conduct is prohibited:

* abusive, threatening, or discriminatory behaviour; 
* harassment of staff, guests, or other users; 
* violence or disorderly conduct; 
* vandalism or intentional damage; 
* activities that create unnecessary safety risks; 
* any unlawful activity. 

InSpace reserves the right to remove any individual whose behaviour is considered unsafe, disruptive, or inappropriate.

### 6.3. **Supervision**

The Client shall ensure that the Event is adequately supervised at all times.

Clients are responsible for setting up the booked Space for their Event unless setup services have been expressly included in the booking.

* remove furniture or equipment from the Facility; 
* dismantle or modify any equipment; 
* tamper with electrical installations or networking equipment; or

Wi-Fi access is provided as a complimentary service for the convenience of Clients and Attendees.

Users shall not use the internet connection to:

* engage in unlawful activities; 
* attempt unauthorised access to systems or networks; 
* distribute malware or malicious software; 
* infringe intellectual property rights; 
* send spam or unsolicited communications; 
* interfere with the operation or security of the network; or 
* engage in any activity that may disrupt other users.

### 7.5. **Electrical Equipment and Power**

Clients intending to use equipment with unusually high electrical power requirements shall notify InSpace before the booking.

InSpace reserves the right to refuse the use of equipment that may:

* overload electrical circuits; 
* damage the Facility; 
* interfere with normal operations; or 
* present a safety risk. 

Power supply is subject to the availability of public electricity and any backup systems operated by InSpace.

### 7.6. **Food and Catering**

Clients may provide refreshments or engage external caterers, subject to prior approval from InSpace.

Cooking within the Facility is prohibited unless expressly authorised in writing.

Clients are responsible for ensuring that food preparation, serving, and disposal are carried out hygienically and without causing damage or disruption to the Facility.

All food waste and catering materials must be removed before the end of the booking unless otherwise agreed.

### 7.7. **Decorations**

Decorations may only be installed with the prior approval of InSpace.

Clients shall not use nails, screws, staples, permanent adhesives, paint, or any other material that may damage the Facility.

The use of candles, fireworks, smoke machines, confetti, glitter, or other hazardous or difficult-to-clean materials is prohibited unless expressly authorised.

All decorations must be removed before the booking ends.

### 7.8. **Signage and Branding**

Temporary event signage, banners, or branded materials may be displayed only within the booked Space and with prior approval from InSpace.

No signage may be attached to the exterior of the Facility or to any permanent structure without written permission.

### 7.9. **Storage**

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, InSpace does not provide storage facilities for equipment, materials, or personal belongings before or after a booking.

Items left within the Facility after the booking may be treated as lost property in accordance with this Policy.

### 7.10. **Lost Property**

Clients are responsible for their personal belongings and equipment throughout the duration of their booking.

InSpace accepts no responsibility for loss, theft, or damage to personal property.

Items found within the Facility will be retained for up to thirty (30) days where reasonably practicable.

After this period, unclaimed items may be disposed of, donated, or otherwise handled at the discretion of InSpace.

### 7.11. **Facility Care**

Clients shall use the Facility with reasonable care and shall not:

* deface or damage walls, floors, ceilings, furniture, or equipment; 
* obstruct entrances, exits, corridors, or emergency routes; 
* interfere with security or safety equipment; 
* misuse restroom or kitchen facilities; or 
* undertake any activity likely to reduce the cleanliness, safety, or functionality of the Facility.


## 8. **Safety, Compliance, and Restrictions**  
### 8.1. **Safety and Emergency Procedures**

The Client shall ensure that all Attendees comply with all applicable safety requirements and any reasonable instructions issued by InSpace staff.

Emergency exits, fire extinguishers, first aid equipment, electrical panels, and other safety equipment must remain accessible and shall not be obstructed, removed, or tampered with at any time.

In the event of an emergency, all persons shall immediately follow the directions of InSpace staff or emergency responders.

### 8.2. **Compliance with InSpace Policies**

Clients shall comply with this Policy, the InSpace Terms & Conditions, and all other applicable InSpace policies, including but not limited to the:

* Safety Policy;

Smoking, vaping, the use of e-cigarettes, the possession or consumption of illegal drugs, and any unlawful or controlled substances are strictly prohibited within the Facility.

### 8.4. **Noise**

Clients shall ensure that noise levels remain reasonable and do not interfere with neighbouring businesses, other occupants, or surrounding properties.

InSpace reserves the right to require the volume of music, microphones, or other sound equipment to be reduced where necessary.

Persistent excessive noise may result in suspension or termination of the Event.

### 8.5. **Security**

Clients are responsible for the security of their personal belongings, equipment, and materials brought into the Facility.

Clients shall not interfere with security systems, CCTV equipment, access control systems, or security procedures.

### 8.6. **Prohibited Activities**

The following activities are prohibited unless expressly authorised in writing by InSpace:

* illegal activities; 
* gambling; 
* possession of weapons; 
* use of fireworks or explosives; 
* hazardous chemicals or materials; 
* open flames; 
* activities likely to damage the Facility; 
* activities that present unreasonable health or safety risks.
