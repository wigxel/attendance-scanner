## How to Book InSpace

To book InSpace, send an email to **business@inspace.ng** with your preferred date, time, type of event (e.g., meeting, workshop, photoshoot), and expected number of attendees. InSpace will respond with availability, pricing, and payment details. Your booking is only confirmed after you receive written confirmation and make the required payment.

---

## 1. Scope

This policy governs the rental and use of InSpace located at **71 Rumuokuta Road, Mgbuoba, Obio/Akpor, Rivers State**. It applies to all individuals and organizations (“Clients”) who book, rent, or use the space for meetings, photoshoots, small events, workshops, and trainings.

## 2. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to ensure that the space is used safely, responsibly, and respectfully. It aims to protect the property, provide a consistent experience for all clients, and clarify the terms and conditions of rental.

## 3. Booking and Payment

### 3.1 Booking

All bookings must be requested by sending an email to **business@inspace.ng**. Bookings are only confirmed after written confirmation is sent to the client and the required payment has been received.

### 3.2 Deposit and Fees

- A deposit of 50% of the total rental fee is required to secure the booking for events where no discount has been applied and the total amount is **NGN 30,000** or more.  
- For bookings where a discount has been applied, or where the total amount is less than **NGN 30,000**, full payment is required at the time of booking.  
- The remaining balance (where applicable) must be paid no later than **3 days** before the rental date.  
- After each payment is made, the client must send proof of payment (receipt or transfer confirmation) to **business@inspace.ng** so that the payment can be verified and recorded.  
- Additional charges may apply for overtime, damages, cleaning, exceeding maximum capacity, or other costs arising from the client’s use of the space.

### 3.3 Cancellations and Refunds

For paid bookings (i.e., not free or discounted events):

- Cancellations made at least **7 days** before the rental date are subject to a **10% cancellation fee**. The remaining 90% of the amount paid will be refunded.  
- Cancellations made less than **7 days** before the rental date will result in a full forfeit of the amount paid (no refund).  
- No-shows are not eligible for refunds.

For free or discounted events, cancellation terms will be communicated at the time of booking and may differ from the above.

---

## 4. Use of Space

### 4.1 Access, Hours, Check‑In and Check‑Out

Standard event hours at InSpace are **10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.**

- Clients may not schedule events to start before 10:00 a.m. or end after 5:00 p.m., except where expressly agreed in writing by InSpace and subject to availability and any additional fees.  
- Clients may arrive and begin setup up to **1 hour before** their booked start time, but not earlier than 10:00 a.m.  
- All attendees, vendors, and belongings must be out of the space by the booked end time (or by 5:00 p.m., whichever is earlier), unless overtime has been approved.

Overtime, where approved, will be charged at a rate of **NGN 2,000 per hour** and is subject to availability. Any part of an hour used beyond the booked time may be rounded up and charged as a full hour, at the discretion of InSpace.

### 4.2 Permitted Uses

The space may be used for the following purposes:

- Meetings  
- Photoshoots  
- Small events  
- Workshops  
- Trainings  

All activities must comply with applicable laws and regulations. Any unusual or high-impact activities require prior written approval.

### 4.3 Capacity

The maximum occupancy of the space is **35 people**. Clients must not exceed this limit under any circumstances. Exceeding the maximum capacity may result in additional fees and/or immediate termination of the event at the discretion of InSpace, without refund.

## 5. Responsibilities of the Client

### 5.1 Conduct

Clients are responsible for the behavior of all attendees, vendors, and guests. All users must conduct themselves respectfully and avoid activities that may cause disturbance, damage, or safety risks.

### 5.2 Setup and Cleanup

Clients are responsible for their own setup and cleanup and must:

- Arrange furniture and equipment as needed for their event.  
- Ensure the space is returned to its original condition at the end of the rental.  
- Remove all personal items, decorations, and waste.

Excessive cleaning required after use may incur additional fees.

### 5.3 Damage and Loss

Clients are liable for any damage to the space, furnishings, fixtures, or equipment that occurs during the rental period. The cost of repair or replacement will be charged to the client.

## 6. Equipment, Amenities, and Decorations

### 6.1 Included Amenities

The rental includes use of the space and any standard furnishings provided (e.g., available tables and chairs, basic lighting, and restroom access) as specified in the booking confirmation.

InSpace does **not** provide a sound system or kitchen facilities. Clients must make their own arrangements for any sound equipment or catering services, subject to the venue’s approval and building rules.

### 6.2 Wi‑Fi and Power

InSpace provides complimentary Wi‑Fi for client use on an “as is” basis with no guarantee of speed or availability. There is no specific limit on high‑power equipment; however, InSpace may require prior notice for unusually heavy electrical loads to ensure safety and building compliance.

### 6.3 Decorations and Fixtures

InSpace must approve any decorations or fixtures in advance. Use of nails, screws, or adhesives that may damage surfaces, as well as open flames or any hazardous materials, is prohibited. All decorations must be fully removed by the client at the end of the booking.

## 7. Safety, Compliance, and Restrictions

### 7.1 Safety and Emergency

Clients must keep exits clear at all times and comply with all fire, safety, and occupancy regulations. Emergency exits, fire extinguishers, and other safety equipment must not be blocked or tampered with.

### 7.2 Smoking, Alcohol, and Substances

Smoking of any kind (including e‑cigarettes or vaping), consumption or sale of alcoholic beverages, and use or possession of any illegal or controlled substances are strictly prohibited anywhere on the premises, including all indoor areas and any adjacent outdoor spaces under the control of InSpace.

Any violation of this rule may result in immediate termination of the booking without refund, removal from the premises, and/or reporting to the appropriate authorities where applicable.

### 7.3 Noise

Noise levels must be kept within reasonable limits and compliant with local noise ordinances. InSpace reserves the right to request volume reduction or to terminate the event if noise becomes excessive or results in complaints.

### 7.4 Age and Supervision

There is no specific age limit for events at InSpace. However, clients remain fully responsible for the safety and conduct of all attendees, including minors. InSpace does not provide supervision for children or vulnerable persons.
