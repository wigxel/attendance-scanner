## Special Case 
### Overnight subscribers
Overnight subscribers may access InSpace facilities between 6:00pm and 8:00am as part of their night-time subscription. Overnight subscribers are expected to arrive at the space between 6:00pm and 8:00pm; arrivals outside this window will not be granted access for that night. Access during these hours is solely for quiet work and rest. 

Sleeping is permitted only in designated areas and must not interfere with other members’ use of the space. For safety and security, overnight subscribers must check in with management on arrival each day, ensure all external doors are properly secured after entry or exit, and refrain from granting access to non-subscribers outside regular hours.

InSpace reserves the right to revoke overnight access at any time in response to misuse, disturbance, or violation of any house rule.
